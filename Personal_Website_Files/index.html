"Path of Ants" Android Application Game

"Path of Ants" is a fantastic Android Application that challenges your eye to hand coordination, as well as your decision making process. It's a simple concept where you save the ant from entering the restricted space by making sure that the ant stays on the designated path. The game features a high FPS refresh rate, intuitive GUI, and stunning graphics. Challenge your friends, and save the ants!
